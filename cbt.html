CBT Exam Platform Setup & Management

Our Computer-Based Testing (CBT) Exam Platform provides a seamless, secure, and scalable solution for schools to conduct online assessments. Here’s how we help you set up and manage your CBT exams:

  • Platform Setup: We handle the full deployment and configuration of the CBT platform tailored to your school’s needs.
  • Question Bank Management: Easily upload, organize, and categorize questions for various subjects and classes.
  • Exam Scheduling: Schedule exams with flexible timing, duration, and access controls for different student groups.
  • Student Access: Secure student logins and real-time monitoring to ensure exam integrity.
  • Automated Grading: Instant grading and result generation for objective questions, with manual review options for subjective answers.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Comprehensive performance analytics and downloadable reports for teachers and administrators.
  • Support & Training: Full onboarding, training, and ongoing support for your staff and students.
